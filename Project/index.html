Delivery:

We work without holidays and weekends! Customers from Ukraine can receive their order within 5-6 days, depending on the location of the settlement and the chosen delivery method. Delivery is possible to those settlements where Novaya poshta or Ukrposhta branches operate. Orders over UAH 500 with delivery to Ukrposhta branches are delivered free of charge. You can see the available methods, exact terms and cost of delivery during checkout in the order basket, after selecting the city of delivery.

Payment:

Cash / card:

The purchase can be paid in cash or by card upon delivery of the order to the post office. A sales receipt is attached to each order.

By bank transfer:

If you decide to pay for your order by bank transfer, you must specify the form of payment "Bank transfer" when placing the order. When the order is placed, our manager will contact you to confirm the order and send the invoice.
